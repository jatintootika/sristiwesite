\## 🌸 About This Project



Yeh ek \*\*3D Animated Sorry Website\*\* hai jo maine (Jatin) ne Sristi ke liye banayi hai.  

Ismein \*\*3D floating hearts\*\*, \*\*animated stars\*\*, \*\*love letters\*\*, aur \*\*autoplay music\*\* hai.



> \*\*Purpose:\*\* Sristi ko sorry bolna aur batana ki wo meri duniya hai! 💝



\---



\## ✨ Features



\- 🎵 \*\*Auto-Play Music\*\* — "Ishq Wala Love" background mein bajta hai

\- 💝 \*\*3D Floating Hearts\*\* — 80+ animated 3D hearts (Three.js)

\- ⭐ \*\*Particle Stars\*\* — 800+ twinkling stars in background

\- 💌 \*\*Sorry Letters Page\*\* — 5 heartfelt apology letters

\- 🎁 \*\*Surprise Page\*\* — Memory gallery, playlist, promises

\- ⏳ \*\*Love Timer\*\* — Counts time since we fell in love

\- 📱 \*\*Fully Responsive\*\* — Mobile, Tablet, Desktop sab pe chalta hai

\- 🎛️ \*\*Music Toggle\*\* — Top-left button se gaana on/off

\- 🖱️ \*\*Mouse Interactive\*\* — Camera follows your mouse movement
